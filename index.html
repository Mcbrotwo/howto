White Bread

Ingredients

  • 11 c. flour
  • 2 T. yeast
  • 6 T. sugar
  • 6 T. oil
  • 4T salt
  • 1 c. milk
  • 3 c. water
  • Equipment: kettle, large mixing bowl, large mixing spoon(wooden), 4 cup glass measuring cup, plastic wrap, dish towel, baking sheet

Steps

  1. Put water in a kettle and turn on to heat up.
  2. Put 4 cups of flour and all other dry ingredients into a very large bowl and stir to combine.
  3. Pour oil and milk into a large glass measuring cup. if you dont have a large enough measuring cup, combine ingredients in a pot
  4. Start adding the 3 cups of hot water. When you only have about a half a cup left, feel the water and keep stiring and feeling the mixed liquids as you add the water if it's too hot, add a little cold water if it's to cold, add more of the hot water it should be hot enough that you feel a reaction to sticking in your finger but not enough that you will want to pull your finger out
  5. Mix the liquids into the flour with a large spoon.
  6. pour remaining flour into the bowl, stiring as you go along. When its to hard add flour to your spoon and scrap it clean. Start kneading
  7. stop adding flour or add more flour to get a consistency where it's no longer sticking to your fingers
  8. knead about ten minutes You can't overknead when you are doing it by hand. you lift the bread onto its end, press down, turn the bowl, and repeat.
  9. stop when it's satiny and it springs back right away when you press down on it.
  10. pour oil around edge of the bowl. Spin the dough around in the oil to cover the dough and the bowl with oil
  11. cover the bowl in plastic wrap and then a towel
  12. if you are in a hurry, put it in a warm place.
  13. after it has doubled in size, punch it down and form it how you'd like to cook it: loaf, bread sticks, rolls, etc.
  14. let it rise another 40 minutes before baking at 400 degree in the oven for 20 minutes.

Red lentil Soup

Ingredients
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 1/2 c. red lentils
  • 1 T. Vegeta
  • salt and pepper as desired
  • Equipment: soup pot, stiring spoon, sharp knife, grater

Steps

  1. grate two carrots
  2. finely dice a medium onion.
  3. saute the onion in a splotch of oil in the pot.
  4. add seven cups of water, the carrots, the lentils
  5. season with vegeta
  6. bring to a boil then bring to the lowest shimmer
  7. it will take about an hour for the lentils to cook
